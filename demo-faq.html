Frequently Asked Questions

HOW DOES THE BILLING WORK?

We invoice for upcoming service. For example, if your invoice is dated June 1st, it will cover internet service for the month of June. All monthly invoices are due 15 days from the invoice date, unless otherwise noted.

WE ARE ONLY SEASONAL RESIDENTS; CAN WE PUT OUR ACCOUNT ON HOLD?

Yes. Monthly customers can put their account on hold for up to four (4) months. Please email us within 7 days of when you would like to start your hold nad 7 days from when you would like it turned back on.

THE PREVIOUS HOMEOWNERS LEFT THEIR CURRENT EQUIPMENT; DO WE STILL PAY THE FULL INSTALLATION?

No. If equipment is already at the home the cost of installation is only $75.
